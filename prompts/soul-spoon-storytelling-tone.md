# Soul Spoon Storytelling Tone

Generate emotional, healing, and food-focused storytelling scripts.

## Prompt
"Write a story for a [cooking or emotional healing] video:
- Warm, heartfelt tone
- Include sensory details
- Incorporate life lesson or takeaway
- Call-to-action to try recipe or reflect"

## Example Use
- Healing recipes
- Comfort food stories
- Emotional storytelling videos
