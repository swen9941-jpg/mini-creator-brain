# AI Truth Bomb Prompt

Generate bold, curiosity-driven AI insight videos.

## Prompt
"Write a short, punchy AI truth bomb video script that:
- Reveals a surprising fact about AI or technology
- Creates curiosity and wonder
- Is under 3 minutes
- Includes a call-to-action to comment or share"

## Example Use
- “AI is learning faster than humans, but here’s what it won’t tell you…”
- “5 AI tools you didn’t know could change your life”
